Fortnite

Fortnite for PS4 is a massively popular online multiplayer game developed by Epic Games. In this game, you'll join a community of players from around the world in a battle royale-style competition, where the last person or team standing wins.The gameplay mechanics of Fortnite are fast-paced and engaging, with an emphasis on strategy and survival. You'll start each match by jumping out of a flying bus and landing on an island filled with weapons, resources, and other players. From there, you'll scavenge for weapons and materials, build structures for cover, and battle other players to be the last one standing. One of the standout features of Fortnite is its building system. You can gather resources from the environment and use them to build walls, ramps, and other structures for defense and mobility.
